Description : Broadcom BCM943362WCD6 mounted on a Broadcom BCM9WCD1EVAL1 evaluation board

  WLAN    : USI WM-N-BM-02 SiP (BCM43362 Wi-Fi)

  USB-serial UART interface

  Reset button
  Module current monitor

     - 1 x Thermistor
     - 1 x 8Mbit serial flash
